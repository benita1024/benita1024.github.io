ABOUT BENITA

Benita Besa, an undergraduate student at Duke, possesses a vision to inspire change and drive innovation. At the heart of her journey is a passion for technology, finance, research, and entrepreneurship. These fields, she believes, are the keystones for engineering a future that not only embraces the rapid advancements of our digital era but also addresses the pressing challenges of our time with sustainable solutions. Her diverse experiences reflect a commitment to exploring new frontiers, from teaching fundamental computer skills to pioneering research that addresses global challenges. Through a lens of curiosity and a spirit of collaboration, she aims to contribute to a future where she utilizes technology to build a more inclusive and innovative world.

CIVIC ENGAGEMENT

"My academic pursuits are intertwined with a strong dedication to social activism and community engagement. Beyond the classroom, I am heavily involved in volunteering initiatives and hold a deep-seated belief in the importance of giving back to our communities. As a member of the United Nations Association (UNA-NCA), I have had the privilege of participating in advocacy efforts on Capitol Hill, lobbying for increased funding and support for the UN's vital humanitarian and peacekeeping missions. In December of 2022, I had the honor of delivering a speech at the annual UNA-NCA Human Rights Awards ceremony held at the U.S. Capitol. This experience was profoundly impactful, allowing me to amplify the voices of marginalized communities and advocate for human rights on a global stage."
